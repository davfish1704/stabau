Our Services

Industrial Scaffolding

Comprehensive scaffolding solutions for shutdowns, turnarounds, and continuous maintenance operations. We provide engineering, installation, and dismantling services for refineries, petrochemical plants, and power generation facilities. Our scaffolding systems meet international safety standards and support complex industrial access requirements.

Trade Fair Construction

Professional trade fair and exhibition stand construction services. From concept development to on-site installation and dismantling, we deliver impressive presentation spaces that showcase your brand effectively. Our experienced teams ensure timely completion and flawless execution for your exhibitions and events.

Drywall Construction

Complete drywall installation services for industrial facilities, office spaces, and control rooms. We specialize in fire-rated partitions, acoustic walls, and cleanroom construction. Our qualified teams deliver precise installations that meet technical specifications and building regulations.

Industrial Demolition

Safe and efficient demolition services for industrial structures, process equipment removal, and decommissioning projects. We handle everything from selective dismantling to complete facility demolition, always prioritizing safety, environmental compliance, and waste management.

Shutdown & Turnaround Support

Rapid mobilization and professional execution for planned shutdowns and turnaround projects. Our experienced crews work around the clock to minimize downtime, providing mechanical support, insulation work, and general construction services during critical shutdown periods.

Heavy Industrial Construction

Structural steel erection, concrete work, piping support systems, and heavy industrial construction for large-scale projects. We support EPC contractors with skilled manpower and proven execution capabilities for refineries, chemical plants, and industrial facilities worldwide.
